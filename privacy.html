Privacy Policy

Keystone Environmental respects your privacy. We collect only the information you voluntarily provide to us in order to respond to inquiries and deliver our services.

Voluntarily provided information may include your name, email address, phone number, or other contact details submitted through forms, service communications, or direct contact with our team.

SMS Communications

If you provide your mobile phone number or initiate contact with us via text message, we may use your number to send service-related SMS communications such as appointment confirmations, scheduling updates, service notifications, or customer support messages.

You may opt out of SMS communications at any time by replying “STOP” to any message. Message and data rates may apply.

We do not sell, rent, or share mobile phone numbers with third parties for their marketing purposes.
